# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

Wolkvorm is committed to providing a welcoming and inclusive environment for everyone.
We follow the **Contributor Covenant v2.1** as our community standard.

Full text: [https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/)

## Reporting

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, please report it by emailing:

**conduct@wolkvorm.com**

All reports will be reviewed and investigated promptly and fairly.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying and enforcing standards of
acceptable behavior and will take appropriate and fair corrective action in
response to any behavior that they deem inappropriate.
