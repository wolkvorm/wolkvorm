## Responsibilities

Maintainers are responsible for:

- Reviewing and merging pull requests
- Triaging issues
- Releasing new versions
- Ensuring code quality and security
- Guiding the project roadmap

## Becoming a Maintainer

Contributors who have made significant and sustained contributions to Wolkvorm may be invited to become maintainers. The process is described in [GOVERNANCE.md](GOVERNANCE.md).
