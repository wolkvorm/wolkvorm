⚡ Why Wolkvorm

Infrastructure Shouldn't
Require a PhD in HCL

Terraform is powerful but complex. Wolkvorm turns every resource into a simple form — fill, click, deploy.

📝

No Code Required

Stop writing verbose HCL files. Just fill out a form with your desired configuration and Wolkvorm handles the rest.

🔄

Plan & Apply Built-In

See exactly what will change before applying. Real-time streaming logs show every step of your deployment.

📊

Visual Infrastructure

Interactive graph shows all resources and their relationships. See how your VPC connects to subnets, security groups, and EC2 instances.

🚀 How It Works

Three Steps to Deploy

From zero to deployed infrastructure in minutes, not hours.

1

Choose a Resource

Pick from 25+ AWS services — EC2, EKS, S3, RDS, Lambda, VPC, and more.

2

Fill the Form

Configure your resource with guided form fields. Smart defaults and validations prevent mistakes.

3

Deploy

Click apply and watch real-time logs. Wolkvorm generates Terragrunt HCL and manages state automatically.

✨ Features

Everything You Need to
Manage Infrastructure

Wolkvorm isn't just forms — it's a complete infrastructure management platform.

📋

Form-Based Deployment

No HCL, no YAML — just human-friendly forms. Auto-generates Terragrunt configurations behind the scenes.

🔗

Resource Graph

Visual dependency graph showing how your resources connect. See VPCs, subnets, security groups, and instances in one view.

💰

Cost Dashboard

Track infrastructure costs across all your resources. Know exactly what you're spending before and after changes.

✅

Approval Workflows

Require team approval before applying infrastructure changes. Perfect for production environments and compliance.

📜

Audit Logs

Complete history of every action — who created, modified, or destroyed what, and when. Full accountability.

🔐

Policy Engine

Define and enforce naming conventions, required tags, allowed instance types, and security policies across your team.

📦

Import Existing Resources

Already have AWS resources? Import them into Wolkvorm and start managing them through the visual interface.

⚙️

Multi-Environment

Manage dev, staging, and production environments from one dashboard. Consistent configuration across all stages.
